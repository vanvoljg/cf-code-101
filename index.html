Small Pets

Lizards | Guinea Pigs | Horses | Chihuahas

Top 5 reasons to have a small pet:

  1. Small pets are cuter than big pets.
  2. Small pets are much more portable. You can take them to the mall and smuggle them across international borders with relative ease.
  3. You don't have to tell your landlord about small pets. (Sorry, landlords.)
  4. Compared to horses, small pets barely eat anything. Your pet's grocery bill will be much lower than a horse's.
  5. In most contests of skill or agility, small pets win.
